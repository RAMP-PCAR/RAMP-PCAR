<!-- accessibility BEGINS -->
<details class="margin-bottom-medium">
    <summary class="">Accessibility</summary>
    <div>
        <div class="helpSectionBody">
            <p>This page is WCAG 2.0 AA compliant.</p>
            <h4>Keyboard Accessibility:</h4>
            <p>Keyboard functionality is provided as an alternative for users who are unable to use a mouse.
            Use the Tab key to navigate forward to links and controls on the page. Press Shift+Tab to go back one
            step. Use the Enter or Spacebar keys to activate links and controls.</p>
            <h4>Navigation Controls:</h4>
            <p>Zoom Slider: With the Zoom Slider highlighted, use the Up arrow key to zoom in and the Down arrow
            key to zoom out.</p>
            <h4>Datasets/Data Tabs:</h4>
            <p>With either the Datasets or Data tab highlighted, use the Left and Right arrow keys to switch
            between them.</p>
            <h4>Datasets Tab:</h4>
            <p>Within the Datasets tab, use the Up and Down arrow keys to navigate to different layers. With the
            layer handle (6 dots icon) highlighted, use Enter or Spacebar to grab the layer. Grabbed layers are
            indicated with a dark blue background. With the layer grabbed, use the Up or Down arrow keys to
            re-order the layers on the map interface. Press Enter or Spacebar again to release the layer.</p>
        </div>
    </div>
</details>

            <p>
                The advanced toolbar provides utilities that can break accessibility, usually due to their dependance on a mouse-driven input.</p>
            <h3>Usage:</h3>


            <h4>Opening the Toolbar:</h4>
            <p>Select the Advanced button on the main toolbar to open the advanced toolbar.
				Selecting the button a second time will close the advanced toolbar and de-activate any active tools.

                <div class="span- float-right">
                    <div class="module-poster">
                        <img src="./assets/images/help/en/advanced_toolbar/advanced_toolbar_button.jpg" alt="The Toolbar Button" class="" />
                        <p class="position-top- position-right background-dark">
                            The Toolbar Button
                        </p>
                    </div>
                </div>
                <div class="clear"></div>

                </p>
			<h4>Tool Activation:</h4>
            <p>Any tool can be activated by clicking the appropriate button on the advanced toolbar.
				Clicking a second time will de-activate the tool, as will closing the advanced toolbar, or selecting a new tool.
				While active, you may use a tool as many times as you like.
				<div class="span- float-right">
                    <div class="module-poster">
                        <img src="./assets/images/help/en/advanced_toolbar/advanced_toolbar.jpg" alt="The Advanced Toolbar" class="" />

                </p>

            <p>Select the Population button to activate the population tool.
